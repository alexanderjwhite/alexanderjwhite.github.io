About

I help organizations turn artificial intelligence from an experiment into a scalable enterprise capability. As a Principal at CLA, I lead AI and data science strategy for clients across industries—designing governance frameworks, building operating models, and standing up the platforms that move AI initiatives from proof-of-concept to measurable business value.

My work sits at the intersection of technology leadership and business transformation. I partner with C-suite executives and boards to craft AI roadmaps, evaluate build-versus-buy decisions, and create the organizational structures that sustain innovation long after the first model is deployed. With a foundation in statistics (PhD, Biostatistics) and hands-on engineering experience, I bring both strategic vision and technical depth to every engagement.
